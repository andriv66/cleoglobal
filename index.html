What is CLEO?

CLEO Global's mission is one of personal empowerment, to provide a chance for people to be what they want.

The vision of the CLEO project is one of development and promotion of sustainable economic growth through cooperation. It does this by changing the ways individuals interact when exchanging goods and services, by using the blockchain. The scope of the CLEO project is to provide the tools which mobilize social capital, creating collaboration by bringing people together, harnessed by the power of blockchain. Doing so allows CLEO to create a new social fabric which promotes the use of new avenues and new solutions. The CLEO platform overcomes many obstacles to innovation, education and funding by offering a sustainable, scalable infrastructure to address social and economic problems. This offers people plagued by poverty, poor health and limited education a chance to build their own freedom and achieve sustainable and long lasting change.

The underlying belief behind Bitcoin founder Satoshi Nakamoto, is that the power of blockchain could decentralize the wealth of the world. It is this notion that pushes CLEO to build a foundation secured by the TRUSTLESS principle, favoring mathematical certainty over faith in validity by third party.

To build on Nakamoto’s vision, CLEO is building a philanthropic social platform which acts as a meeting point, towards the further decentralized world of crypto currency. This platform will provide the tools which reinforces individual stakeholders and the encourages the blockchain ecosystem by supporting local communities. The use case for buying and selling goods/services on the CLEO blockchain is that the fees are used to pay-it-forward, establishing a network of trust.

How can CLEO help?

  • Give the means to an individual to build their self worth
  • Engage everyone on an equal footing
  • Inspire new ideas to give people control over their own lives
  • Encourage a “pay it forward” incentive via distributed staking and voting that enables fair and transparent funding.
  • Promote social entrepreneurship
  • Enable community based collaboration online and on the ground
  • Provide the tools and resources for individuals and community projects underpinned with charitable co action.
  • Partner with approved charities and work hand and hand with them to maximize charitable potential
